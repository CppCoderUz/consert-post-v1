Named after Islam Karimov under the Ministry of Culture and Tourism of the Republic of Uzbekistan Information about the theater and concert palace of Fergana region.


Fergana Region Theater-Concert Palace named after Islam Karimov of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan It was established on the basis of the decision No. 170 of June 25, 2014 "On organizing the activities of the theater-concert palace of Fergana region". According to the decision of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. PQ-2744 dated January 25, 2017, the theater-concert palace was named after Islam Karimov. On December 6, 2014, the opening ceremony of the Theater-Concert Palace was held. Theater and concert hall, Fergana city It is located at 59 Yuksalish Street. The audience hall of the theater-concert hall is intended for 925 spectators. Theater and concert hall the summer amphitheater is designed for 643 spectators. The palace has all facilities for the audience and artists. Heating and cooling systems are equipped with modern equipment and maintain a warm climate in winter and a moderate climate in summer. For artists, 14 artists per 40 people There is a room with a bathroom and it is furnished with soft furniture. The theater-concert hall has a spiritual room, library, dance and club activities There are 2 small halls, a recording studio and an orchestrator's workshop, an artist's workshop, a workshop. Theater and concert hall All conditions and amenities are created here for art concerts, performances, and film screenings.
